Lesson 3: The は & に Particles

The Topic Marker は

は marks the topic - what we're talking about. It is NOT the subject! The subject (が) may be invisible.
Topic + Statement: Topic は ∅が [statement]

私はアメリカ人だ

= 私は ∅が アメリカ人だ

As for me, (I) am American.

The "Eel Sentence" - Why は ≠ Subject

私はうなぎだ

NOT "I am an eel"!

= As for me, (it) is eel. (ordering food context)

は replaces が but doesn't become が!
The zero pronoun takes over as the subject.
私は ∅が うなぎだ - ∅ = "it" (my order), not "I"

Logical vs Non-Logical Particles

Type Particles Function
Logical が を に Define sentence structure (who does what to whom)
Non-logical は Marks topic only - doesn't affect logic

The Target Marker に

に marks the target/destination of an action.
Throwing/Giving: A が Object を Target に Verb

私がボールをさくらに投げる

I throw the ball at Sakura.

Particle Roles Comparison

Particle Role Question Example
が Subject Who does it? 私が投げる
を Object What is acted on? ボールを投げる
に Target To/at whom? さくらに投げる
は Topic What about? ボールは...

Word Order Doesn't Matter - Particles Do!

私にさくらがボールを投げる

= Sakura throws the ball at me. (さくらが = Sakura is the thrower)

Changing は doesn't change logic!
私はボールをさくらに投げる = As for me, I throw...
ボールは私がさくらに投げる = As for the ball, I throw it...
Same action, different topics.
